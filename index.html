Our Vision and Mission

Google Developer Group (GDG) Kolkata is a community-based focused group for developers on Google and related technologies. We as a community, organize various events on Technologies and the comming up big things and platforms. Our aim is to establish and grow an open community of student and professional developers who are passionate about technology, allowing them to learn, share, and collaborate among themselves. We are working towards that goal through various events such as the ones below.
